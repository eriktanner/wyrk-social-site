DRIPn is built for respectful, non-explicit creator communities. The following principles apply to all users.

Respect and Safety

  • No harassment, threats, or abusive conduct.
  • No adult, explicit, sexual, non-consensual, or exploitative content or monetization.
  • No hate, extremism, or targeted abuse.

Integrity

  • No fraud, scams, impersonation, or deceptive activity.
  • No unauthorized account access or security abuse.
  • No wallet, stored-value, token, crypto, tipping, gambling, sweepstakes, or betting activity.

Enforcement

Policy violations may result in content removal, account restrictions, suspension, or permanent account termination.
